Soccer at
Columbia FC

In my free time I love playing soccer with my semi-professional soccer team based in Columbia, MD.

Soccer holds significant prominence in my life, serving as both a passion and a means of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Beyond the sheer enjoyment derived from playing, the sport plays a pivotal role in keeping me physically fit. Moreover, engaging in soccer acts as a cathartic release, effectively mitigating stressors and enhancing my ability to concentrate on professional responsibilities when I am in the office. This harmonious balance between recreation and well-being contributes substantially to my overall productivity and work-life equilibrium.

Engaging in soccer has significantly contributed to the honing of essential life skills such as leadership and communication, shaping my character for day-to-day challenges. During my four seasons with Columbia FC, our team achieved notable success, reaching three finals in the Division 1 Maryland Majors League and securing victory twice. This journey has not only adorned me with trophies and accolades but has also provided unique opportunities, including a meeting with Baltimore Mayor Brandon Scott. Throughout this experience, I have embraced the dual roles of a leader and a team player, learning valuable lessons in collaboration and effective communication. Gratitude extends to my dedicated teammates and coaches whose support has been instrumental in my personal and athletic growth.

In my leisure moments, I derive immense joy from devoting quality time to my family, an activity that I consider both rejuvenating and profoundly humbling. The bonds forged during these moments serve as a source of inspiration, as my family challenges and guides me in my continuous journey towards becoming a more virtuous Christian. Notably, my mother, portrayed in the image above, stands as a beacon of resilience and determination. Overcoming numerous obstacles, she emerged as an evangelist in Ethiopia during a time when societal norms restricted women from assuming leadership roles within the church. Her life narrative serves as a constant reminder that divine providence is steadfast, underscoring the notion that God is perpetually vigilant over my endeavors. In closing, I share a cherished biblical verse: "Submit to God and be at peace with Him; in this way, prosperity will come to you." - Job 22:21.
